About the club

who we are

Tirta Taruna 66 was founded on 10 November 1966 to promote and improve competitive swimming. For six decades we've consistently developed National and International athletes.

We're a member-run organization — managed by swimmers and swimmers' parents, for swimmers' achievement. That structure is a big part of why TT66 has stayed strong for so long.
